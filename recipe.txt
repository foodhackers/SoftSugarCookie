Soft Sugar Cookie or Snickerdoodle Recipe

* 1/4 Cup Crisco
* 1/2 Stick Butter
* 1 Cup White Sugar
* 1/2 Cup Powdered Sugar
* 1 Tablespoon Vanilla Extract
* 1 Large Egg
* 2 1/2 cup All Purpose Flour
* 3/4 Teaspoon Salt
* 1/2 Teaspoon Baking Soda
* 1/4 Cup Milk
* 1 Pack of Vanilla Pudding (Jello Pack)

Bake at 350 (F) for 10 minutes

*** Snicker Doodle Option ***
Cinnamon sugar mix: cinnamon to sugar ratio -- 1:3

Add mix to small bowl or bag and roll dough in mix prior to baking.
